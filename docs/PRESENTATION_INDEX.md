## 🎯 How to Use This Package

### 📅 One Week Before
1. Read [PRESENTATION.md](PRESENTATION.md) - understand the flow
2. Read [SPEAKER_NOTES.md](SPEAKER_NOTES.md) - learn the details
3. Practice the demo 2-3 times using Slide 5 instructions

### 📅 One Day Before
1. Review [SPEAKER_NOTES.md](SPEAKER_NOTES.md) again
2. Read [QUICK_REFERENCE.md](QUICK_REFERENCE.md) - memorize key numbers
3. Print 2 copies of [EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md](EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md)
4. Print 1 copy of [QUICK_REFERENCE.md](QUICK_REFERENCE.md)
5. Do a full dress rehearsal with timer

### 📅 On Presentation Day
1. Follow [PRESENTATION_CHECKLIST.md](PRESENTATION_CHECKLIST.md) step-by-step
2. Keep [QUICK_REFERENCE.md](QUICK_REFERENCE.md) visible during presentation
3. Use [PRESENTATION.md](PRESENTATION.md) as your slide deck
4. Have [EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md](EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md) ready to hand out

**If you only have 2 hours to prepare:**

   - Read PRESENTATION.md (15 min)

## 🎯 Presentation Structure at a Glance

```
OPENING (30 sec)
   ↓
PROBLEM & SOLUTION (5 min)
   ↓
DEMO ⭐ (5-7 min) ← MOST IMPORTANT
   ↓
BUSINESS VALUE (3-5 min)
   ↓
FUTURE & Q&A (2-3 min)
   ↓
CLOSING ASK (30 sec)
```

**Total Time:** 15-20 minutes

## 🚨 Common Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ Reading slides word-for-word  
❌ Rushing through the demo  
❌ Getting too technical too fast  
❌ Not asking for approval at the end  
❌ Apologizing for minor hiccups

✅ Tell a story  
✅ Let the demo breathe  
✅ Match their technical level  
✅ Clear call to action  
✅ Show confidence

## 📞 What Success Looks Like

### Best Case: Immediate Approval
*"This is great. Let's start with 5 users. When can you begin?"*

**Your response:**
- "Thank you! I can start the pilot this week."
- "I'll send you a kickoff email by [specific time]."
- Hand them EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md
- Follow up email within 1 hour

### Good Case: Conditional Approval
*"I like it, but can you show me [X] first?"*

**Your response:**
- "Absolutely. I can prepare that by [specific date]."
- "Would you like a follow-up meeting or just an email?"
- Note their concern carefully
- Follow up as promised

### Okay Case: Deferred Decision
*"Let me think about this. Give me a few days."*

**Your response:**
- "Of course. What additional information would help?"
- "Should I follow up on [specific date]?"
- Leave EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md with them
- Email within 24 hours thanking them
