# Zoom MCP - Executive Summary
## AI-Powered Meeting Management Integration

---

## 🎯 The Problem

Meeting management consumes **5-10 minutes per meeting** due to:
- Multiple app switches (Calendar → Zoom → Browser)
- Manual scheduling and link generation
- Difficulty finding recordings and participant data
- No natural language interface

**Impact:** Wasted time, reduced productivity, context switching overhead

---

## 💡 The Solution

**Zoom MCP connects Claude AI directly to your Zoom account**

Simply ask Claude:
- *"Show my meetings for today"*
- *"Create a team standup tomorrow at 10am"*
- *"Who attended yesterday's client call?"*
- *"Find last week's project review recording"*

**Result:** Natural language meeting management - no browser, no clicking
